About Me

I'm a creative-minded full-stack developer with a builder's mindset and a passion for well-crafted software.

With a successful career in structural welding, engineering, and project management, I bring deep experience in building systems that solve real-world, complex problems. I specialize in designing intuitive web applications that align with business goals and enhance the user experience.

I code with persistence and precision, drawing on years of working under pressure, solving problems creatively, and leading collaborative, cross-functional teams. My projects reflect a hands-on approach to development — balancing design sensibility with clean architecture and scalable infrastructure.

Recently, I completed General Assembly's full-time Software Engineering Immersive, where I built full-stack applications using React, Node.js, Django, MongoDB, and PostgreSQL. I'm excited to bring my builder's brain, leadership instincts, and user-first mindset to software teams focused on purposeful products.
